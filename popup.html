How to use

Start typing in any text field. The extension will automatically show a Tab indicator when suggestions are available. Press Tab to accept the AI-generated medical text completion.

Note: Works with standard web forms, EMR systems, medical platforms, and Google Docs. In Google Docs, suggestions are inserted via clipboard.
